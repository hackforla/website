Getting Started

Hack for LA brings together civic-minded volunteers to build digital products, programs and services with community partners and local government to address issues in the LA region. Please review this Getting Started guide to kickstart your journey with Hack for LA.

Self Onboarding

Onboarding Video

  1. Watch our onboading video where you will hear a brief introduction to Code for America and Hack for LA, meet members of active projects, and hear about the intended impact of all projects and the roles these projects are looking to fill.
  2. Read the Hack for LA Code of Conduct.
  3. Complete the New Member Survey.

Slack

  1. Self-invite to the Hack for LA Slack team.
  2. Join the Slack channel specific to your Hack Night.
    1. Downtown LA (DTLA): #dtla-hacknight
    2. Westside (Santa Monica): #westside-hacknight
    3. South LA (SOLA): #sola-hacknight

Projects

  1. Review the projects listed in the projects section to determine which one you would like to know more about.
  2. Click to the project’s home page to locate and review the project-specific getting started guide.
  3. Join the project’s Slack channel.

GitHub

  1. Sign up for a GitHub account.
  2. Set up two-factor authentication.
  3. Mark your Hack for LA organization membership public.
