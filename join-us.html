Join Us

We bring together civic-minded volunteers to build digital products, programs and services with community partners and local government to address issues in our LA region and to share these effective processes, practices, and code with the larger civic tech community.

Hack for LA is a project of Code for America and is its official Los Angeles chapter.

Advice Us

Hack for LA needs Advisors in a variety of areas. These roles are flexible and can be short term or ongoing. If you deeply experienced in a specific area such as Fundraising, Marketing, Scaleable System Architecture, Deversity Equity and Inclusion innatives, etc. we are interested in talking to you.

How to get involved:

  1. Join our slack (if you are comfortable using slack)
  2. Connect with out Executive Director, Bonnie Wolfe
  3. Send and email to our Civic Opportunity team

Volunteer with Us

Hack for LA projects are currently recruiting for activists, coders, designers, researchers, testers, business analysts, project/product managers, data scientists, dev ops, writers, and marketers of all experiences and skill levels. Everyone is welcome!

We are a Do-ocracy, we are not a school, yet everyone here is learning something, growing their experiences, porfolios and ability to work on inter-disicplinary teams.

Partner with Us

The more you tell us about yourself, the better we can match you with the right resources.
